Maha Puja

Grand Worship Ceremony

Maha Puja is an elaborate worship ceremony performed with great devotion and traditional Vedic rituals. This sacred ceremony includes offerings of flowers, fruits, and prayers to the deities.

Highlights:

  • Traditional Vedic chanting and mantras
  • Sacred offerings and abhishek
  • Community participation and blessings
  • Prasad distribution to all devotees

Chopda Pujan

Hindu New Year Business Blessing

Chopda Pujan is a special ceremony performed during Diwali to worship account books and business records, seeking blessings for prosperity and success in the coming year.

Significance:

  • Marks beginning of Hindu financial year
  • Blessings for business prosperity
  • Worship of Goddess Lakshmi and Lord Ganesha
  • Community prayers for success and abundance

Annakut

Mountain of Food Offering

Annakut, meaning "mountain of food," is a grand festival where hundreds of vegetarian dishes are prepared and offered to the deities. This festival celebrates the first day of the Hindu New Year.

Features:

  • Over 500 varieties of vegetarian dishes prepared
  • Beautiful artistic arrangement of food offerings
  • Celebration of Hindu New Year (Bestu Varas)
  • Community feast and prasad distribution

Diwali

Festival of Lights

Diwali is one of the most celebrated Hindu festivals, symbolizing the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. Our mandir hosts grand celebrations with lights, decorations, and cultural programs.

Celebrations Include:

  • Thousands of diyas and decorative lights
  • Cultural performances and music programs
  • Traditional rangoli and decorations
  • Special aarti and prayers
  • Community dinner and sweets distribution

Pramukh Swami Maharaj

1921 - 2016

The Epitome of Spirituality

Pramukh Swami Maharaj was the fifth spiritual successor of Bhagwan Swaminarayan and served as the guru and Pramukh of BAPS from 1950 to 2016. His life was a living example of faith, devotion, and selfless service.

His Legacy

  • Inspired and consecrated over 1,100 mandirs worldwide
  • Initiated over 1,000 sadhus into the spiritual path
  • Promoted peace, harmony, and humanitarian service globally
  • Guided millions on the path of righteousness and devotion
  • His mantra: "In the joy of others, lies our own"

His Message

"In the joy of others, lies our own. In the progress of others rests our own. In the good of others abides our own."

Mahant Swami Maharaj

Current Spiritual Leader

The Sixth Spiritual Successor

Mahant Swami Maharaj is the sixth spiritual successor of Bhagwan Swaminarayan and the current guru and Pramukh of BAPS. He was ordained as the spiritual leader in 2016, following the divine wish of Pramukh Swami Maharaj.

His Life

  • Born as Keshavjivandas Patel on September 13, 1933
  • Initiated as a sadhu in 1955 by Yogiji Maharaj
  • Served devotedly under Yogiji Maharaj and Pramukh Swami Maharaj
  • Known for his humility, devotion, and spiritual wisdom
  • Continues the mission of peace and spirituality worldwide

His Guidance

"Stay connected to God and the guru, and you will never be alone. Their grace and blessings will always be with you."
